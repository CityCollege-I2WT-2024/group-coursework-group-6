"Mega Star Reportedly Buys $50M Haunted Mansion—But There's a Twist"

Uploaded 6th of December, 2024 by Sophia Hart

In one of the strangest celebrity purchases of the year, Oscar-winning actor Ethan Greer has reportedly shelled out a whopping $50 million for a sprawling Gothic mansion in upstate New York—known locally as The Whispering Manor.

The estate, built in the 1800s, has a reputation for being haunted, with previous owners abandoning it due to unexplained phenomena.

But here's where it gets even juicier: Ethan's ex, singer Jenna Sky, is rumored to have warned him against the purchase, citing a past incident where she experienced terrifying visions during a visit to the mansion years ago. Despite her warning, Ethan seems unbothered.

His cryptic comment? I'm not afraid of a few ghosts. I'm more afraid of living a boring life. Sources say the actor plans to use the property as a retreat for writing his debut novel.
