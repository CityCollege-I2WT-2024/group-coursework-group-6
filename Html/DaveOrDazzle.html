At Tokyo on August 2027

Dave Or Dazzle The Dolphin

In the heart of the human Olympics, a dolphin named Dazzle disguised himself as a human using the name "Dave" with a little trick called sunglasses and a hat.

He entered the men's 100-meter freestyle, diving in with a backflip that left everyone baffled. Dave swam with the grace of a dolphin, leaving the human swimmers far behind.

At the finish, he leaped out of the water, did a spin, and touched the wall, shattering records. The crowd cheered, but officials, suspecting something fishy, asked to see his identification.

Dave, in the heat of the moment, revealed his true identity. Laughter ensued, and he was awarded an honorary medal for his performance.

The Olympic Committee banned dolphins from swimming events, but Dazzle returned to the sea, his medal proudly on display, showing that sometimes, the biggest splash is made by breaking the rules.
